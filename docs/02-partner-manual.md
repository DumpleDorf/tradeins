## 1. Welcome

Welcome to the **Tesla Certified Trade-In Wholesale Portal**. As an approved wholesale partner, you can browse available certified trade-in vehicles and reserve vehicles for purchase.

<div class="note">
<strong>Important:</strong> All reservations are subject to final approval by Tesla. No sale is confirmed until you receive written confirmation from Tesla.
</div>

---

## 2. Logging in

### 2.1 Access the partner login page

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Safari recommended)
2. Go to: **https://teslatradeins.com.au/login/partner**
3. Enter your **email address** and **password**
4. Click **Sign in**

> Partners must be **invited by Tesla** — there is no self-registration. If you do not have an account, contact your Tesla representative.

### 2.2 Forgot your password

1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link
4. Follow the link to set a new password

---

## 3. Browsing inventory

After logging in, you will see the **Available Inventory** page.

### 3.1 Vehicle cards

Each vehicle card shows:

- Vehicle photo
- Year, make, and model
- Mileage
- Condition grade (1–5)
- Indicative list price (AUD)

Click any card to view full details.

### 3.2 Filtering vehicles

Use the **Filters** panel on the left to narrow results:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Make | e.g. Tesla |
| Model | e.g. Model 3 |
| Year from / to | Year range |
| Mileage from / to | Kilometre range |
| Condition (1–5) | Vehicle condition grade |
| Price from / to | Indicative price range (AUD) |

Click **Apply Filters** to update results.

### 3.3 Sorting

Use the **Sort by** dropdown:

- **Newest** — most recently listed first
- **Price: Low to High**
- **Price: High to Low**
- **Mileage** — lowest mileage first

### 3.4 Pagination

If many vehicles are listed, use **Previous** and **Next** at the bottom of the grid to browse additional pages.

---

## 4. Viewing a vehicle

Click a vehicle card to open the **Vehicle Detail** page.

### 4.1 Information shown

- Full photo gallery (click image to zoom)
- Year, make, model
- Indicative list price
- Mileage, condition grade
- Exterior and interior colour
- VIN (Vehicle Identification Number)
- Available from date
- Full written description

<div class="note">
List prices are <strong>indicative only</strong> and are not transactional. Final pricing is confirmed by Tesla upon approval.
</div>

---

## 5. Reserving a vehicle

### 5.1 How to reserve

1. Open the vehicle detail page
2. Click **Reserve This Vehicle**
3. Read the confirmation message carefully
4. Click **Confirm Reservation**

### 5.2 What happens when you reserve

| Action | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Vehicle status changes to **Reserved** | Immediately hidden from all other partners |
| Email sent to Tesla | Tesla team notified with your details and vehicle info |
| Your reservation recorded | Visible in **My Reservations** |

<div class="note">
<strong>One partner per vehicle:</strong> Once you reserve a vehicle, no other partner can see or reserve it. If Tesla rejects your reservation, the vehicle returns to available inventory.
</div>

---

## 6. My Reservations

Access **My Reservations** from the top navigation or inventory page.

### 6.1 Reservation statuses

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Pending Approval** | Tesla is reviewing your reservation |
| **Approved** | Tesla has approved — a representative will contact you |
| **Rejected** | Tesla did not approve; reason may be shown |

### 6.2 After approval

When Tesla approves your reservation:

- You will receive a **confirmation email**
- A Tesla representative will contact you to finalise the sale
- The reservation status changes to **Approved**

> This email constitutes written confirmation of Tesla's approval.

---

## 7. What you cannot do

As a partner, the following restrictions apply:

- You **cannot** see other partners' reservations or activity
- You **cannot** reserve a vehicle that is already reserved
- You **cannot** self-register — accounts are invite-only
- You **cannot** see vehicles that are not in **Available** status
